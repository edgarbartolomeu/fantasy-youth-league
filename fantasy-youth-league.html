❓ Help & Guide

⚽ How to Play

1. Create Your Account

Sign up with your name, team name, and password. Your username must be unique!

2. Pick Your Team (11 Players)

  • Budget: £100 million total
  • Formation: 1 GK, 3-5 DEF, 2-5 MID, 1-3 FWD
  • Squad limit: Maximum 2 players from same team
  • 💡 Use the 🎲 Auto Pick button for instant random selection!

3. Select Your Captain

Go to My Team tab and click the Captain button on your best player. They'll score double points!

4. Confirm Your Team

Click Confirm Team before the gameweek deadline. After the deadline, your team is locked!

5. Watch Your Points Grow

Check the Points tab to see your score. Visit Leaderboard to see your rank!

6. Make Transfers

  • Get 2 free transfers each gameweek
  • Extra transfers cost -4 points each
  • Use chips wisely: Triple Captain (3x points!) or Free Hit (unlimited free transfers)

📊 Points System

Goals

  • Forwards: 6 points per goal
  • Midfielders: 6 points per goal
  • Defenders: 6 points per goal
  • Goalkeepers: 10 points per goal

Assists

All positions: 3 points per assist

Clean Sheet (Team didn't concede)

  • Goalkeepers: +4 points
  • Defenders: +4 points
  • Midfielders: +1 point
  • Forwards: 0 points

Goals Conceded Penalty

GK/DEF only: -1 point for every 2 goals conceded

Example: If your GK concedes 4 goals, they lose 2 points (4 ÷ 2 = 2)

⭐ Captain Bonus

  • Normal: Captain scores 2x points
  • Triple Captain chip: Captain scores 3x points (one-time use!)

📋 CSV Upload Guide (For Admins)

🌐 NEW: Google Sheets Integration!

Load players and fixtures directly from Google Sheets - no CSV uploads needed! Everyone automatically sees your updates.

Quick Setup:

  1. Create a Google Sheet with your data
  2. File → Share → Publish to web → CSV format
  3. Copy the URL
  4. Edit GOOGLE_SHEETS_CONFIG in the code (line ~1762)
  5. Set enabled: true
  6. Paste your sheet URLs
  7. Save and reload - done!

Example Configuration:

const GOOGLE_SHEETS_CONFIG = {
    enabled: true,
    playersSheet: 'https://docs.google.com/.../pub?output=csv',
    fixturesSheet: 'https://docs.google.com/.../pub?output=csv',
    autoLoadOnStartup: true
};

✅ Benefits: Update your Google Sheet once, everyone sees changes instantly!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I change my team after the deadline?

A: No! Once the gameweek deadline passes, your team is locked. You can make changes for the next gameweek.

Q: What happens if I don't use my free transfers?

A: Free transfers don't carry over. You get 2 fresh transfers each gameweek.

Q: Can I use the same password as someone else?

A: Yes! Passwords can be the same, but usernames must be unique.

Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?

A: Contact your league admin. They can reset your password in the Admin panel.

Q: How do chips work?

A: Each chip can be used once per season:

  • Triple Captain: Your captain scores 3x points instead of 2x for one gameweek
  • Free Hit: Make unlimited transfers for one gameweek with no penalties. Your team reverts next gameweek.

Q: Why can't I pick 3 players from the same team?

A: Squad limit rule! Maximum 2 players per team to keep things fair and diverse.

Q: Do I get points if my player is on the bench?

A: There are no benches in this league - all 11 players score points!

Q: My data disappeared when I refreshed! Help!

A: Your data is stored in your browser. If you cleared browser data or switched devices, your data is gone. Admins should export backups regularly!
